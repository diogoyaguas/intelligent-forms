Intelligent Form Design: Exploiting Unstructured Natural Language Fields

A form is composed of unstructured and structured fields. These fields are poorly filled in by users who formalize contacts in forms, leading to low-quality contacts. The solution involves introducing functionalities in these fields to improve the quality of the information provided by users, allowing for faster downstream processing.

The two examples shown below contain these features.

  • Verify inconsistency between Intention of Contact and Description field;
  • Autocomplete fields of Identification of Economic Operator.
  • Autocomplete Economic Activity, and Competence fields.
